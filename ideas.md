<idea>
  <design_movement>Swiss Style / International Typographic Style</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    - **Objective Clarity**: Data is the hero; no decorative distractions.
    - **Grid-Based Precision**: Everything aligns to a strict mathematical grid.
    - **Asymmetry**: Dynamic balance through asymmetric layouts rather than centering.
    - **Typography as Interface**: Type weight and size define hierarchy, not boxes or borders.
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    - **Stark Contrast**: High-contrast black and white base.
    - **Signal Colors**: Use of a single, bold signal color (e.g., International Orange or Swiss Red) strictly for actionable data and alerts.
    - **Intent**: Evokes trust, precision, and scientific accuracy.
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    - **Split-Screen Asymmetry**: Fixed left sidebar for chat (30%), expansive right area for data visualization (70%).
    - **Modular Grid**: Content cards align perfectly to a 12-column grid with generous gutters.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    - **Grotesque Typography**: Heavy use of Helvetica or similar neo-grotesque fonts.
    - **Thick Rule Lines**: Bold horizontal lines separating sections.
    - **Oversized Numbers**: Key metrics (ROI, Yield) displayed in massive font sizes.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    - **Instant Feedback**: Hover states are sharp and immediate (no fade-ins).
    - **Type-Based Navigation**: Tabs and buttons are text-only with underlines or weight changes.
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    - **Snap Transitions**: Elements snap into place; no ease-in-out curves.
    - **Linear Motion**: Progress bars fill linearly and instantly.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - **Primary**: Inter or Helvetica Now (Bold for headers, Regular for body).
    - **Monospace**: JetBrains Mono for data tables and code snippets.
  </typography_system>
</idea>
</text>

<idea>
  <design_movement>Eco-Brutalism / Digital Garden</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    - **Raw & Organic**: Unrefined edges, visible structures, "digital loam".
    - **Information Density**: High density of data, similar to a bloomberg terminal but for nature.
    - **Tactile Feel**: Textures that mimic paper, soil, or organic matter.
    - **Honest UI**: Default browser inputs styled minimally, exposing the "machine".
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    - **Earthy Palette**: Deep soil browns, moss greens, unbleached paper creams.
    - **High-Vis Accents**: Neon lime or safety orange for critical alerts (like construction markers in a field).
    - **Intent**: Connects the digital tool to the physical reality of farming.
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    - **Collage / Masonry**: Dashboard elements fit together like a puzzle or masonry grid, not strict rows.
    - **Floating Panels**: Chat and context panels float over a textured background.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    - **Monospace Headings**: Headers look like typewriter or terminal output.
    - **Visible Borders**: 1px solid borders on everything, defining clear boundaries.
    - **Grainy Gradients**: Backgrounds have noise/grain to simulate texture.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    - **Mechanical Clicks**: Buttons have distinct "pressed" states (transform: translate).
    - **Draggable Elements**: Users can rearrange their dashboard widgets.
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    - **Frame-by-Frame**: Animations look like stop-motion or low frame rate.
    - **Glitch/Analog**: Subtle analog interference effects on data updates.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - **Primary**: Space Grotesk or Syne (idiosyncratic sans).
    - **Secondary**: Courier Prime or similar typewriter font for data.
  </typography_system>
</idea>

<idea>
  <design_movement>Glassmorphism / Solarpunk Futurism</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    - **Optimistic Future**: Bright, airy, and clean visuals suggesting a high-tech sustainable future.
    - **Translucency**: Layers of frosted glass to show depth and context.
    - **Soft Light**: Diffused shadows and glows, mimicking sunlight.
    - **Fluidity**: Everything flows and connects organically.
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    - **Sky & Leaf**: Gradients of sky blue to fresh leaf green.
    - **Sunlight Gold**: Warm accents for high-value recommendations.
    - **Intent**: Inspires hope and suggests advanced technology working in harmony with nature.
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    - **Card-Based Floating**: Content lives on floating glass cards over a subtle, animated natural background.
    - **Central Focus**: Chat is central but transparent, allowing data to "shine through" or appear alongside.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    - **Frosted Glass**: Backdrop-filter: blur() on panels.
    - **Soft Rounded Corners**: Large border-radius (24px+).
    - **Inner Glows**: Subtle white inner borders to simulate light catching edges.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    - **Fluid Response**: Elements gently scale up on hover.
    - **Parallax**: Background moves slightly slower than foreground cards.
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    - **Smooth Easing**: Long, luxurious ease-out curves.
    - **Shimmer**: Loading states shimmer like light on water.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - **Primary**: Plus Jakarta Sans or Outfit (Geometric, friendly).
    - **Secondary**: DM Sans for body text.
  </typography_system>
</idea>
